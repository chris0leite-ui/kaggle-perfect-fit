x10 x x11 Interaction Discovery

EBM's Round 1 diagnostics discovered that x10 & x11 had the strongest pairwise interaction (mean |score| = 1.60, 2.3x the next). Round 2 EDA confirmed this is a pure multiplicative interaction (F=56.3, p<1e-8, R²=0.258 of residual variance).

Adding x10*x11 to GAM improved it from 4.40 → 3.56 CV MAE — closing 48% of the gap to EBM.
